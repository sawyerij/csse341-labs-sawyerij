Forging Requests
-----------------
1. hostA arp cache empty
	> Fills the entry

2. hostA arp cache already populated
	> Steals the place in the cache

3. hostA arp cache incomplete
	> Fills the incomplete entry

Forging Replies
----------------
1. hostA arp cache empty
	> Nothing happens

2. hostA arp cache already populated
	> Steals the arp entry

3. hostA arp cache incomplete
	> Fills the incomplete entry on hostA

Forging Gratitude
------------------
1. hostA arp cache empty
	> Nothing happens

2. hostA arp cache already populated
	> Steals the arp entry

3. hostA arp cache incomplete
	> Fills the incomplete entry on hostA
